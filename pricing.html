Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise customers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and are SOC 2 compliant. Your data is backed up daily.

Do you offer training?

All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Enterprise plans include on-site training.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel anytime with no penalties. Monthly plans end at the current period. Annual plans can be refunded pro-rata.
